How It Works

Simple, transparent process from project submission to blockchain verification.

1. NGO Submits Project

Environmental organizations submit their carbon offset projects with documentation and evidence.

2. Admin Reviews & Approves

Certified administrators review project details, verify claims, and approve legitimate projects.

3. Blockchain Verification

Approved projects generate NFT tokens on the blockchain, creating immutable proof of carbon credits.
